Beginner Tips for Your First Product

Start Low

Even beginner-friendly devices should start at their lowest setting. Your body needs to learn the sensation before building intensity. The Satisfyer Pro 2 and Bvee Rabbit both have gentle start modes specifically for this purpose.

Explore Lube

Water-based lubricant transforms the experience. A small bottle of your favorite lube is often included with starter products. Don't rush—take time to find a lube that feels natural to you.

Take Your Time

First experiences don't need to be rushed. Set aside time to explore your body, discover what feels good, and build confidence. The Lovehoney Desire's single-button design means no complicated settings to worry about.

Keep It Simple

Avoid overthinking during your first experience. Focus on physical sensations, breathing, and exploration. If the device has modes, start with presets rather than trying to dial in custom patterns.
