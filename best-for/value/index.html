Value Shopping Tips

Check for Sales

Fun Factory and Satisfyer frequently run holiday sales. The Manta often drops to $39 during promotions, and the Pro 2 goes on sale for $89 in October. Sign up for our newsletter for early access to these deals.

Consider Used Carefully

Buy used only from trusted sellers like Amazon Renewed or manufacturer trade-in programs. The Bvee Rabbit's waterproof build means previous owners can thoroughly sanitize. Always check for warranty transferability.

Bundle for Value

Pair a budget wand with a quality lube or use what's included. Many starter kits include lube that's worth $15 alone. The Satisfyer Pro 2 includes a travel case worth $25 on its own.

Warranty Protection

Even budget devices include 1-2 year warranties. Satisfyer offers 2 years, which means they stand behind their quality. If something breaks, the warranty covers replacement—making the effective cost lower.
