Best Products for Travel

Compact, discreet, TSA-friendly, and reliable on the go—perfect for keeping in your luggage

Intimacy Anywhere

Spontaneous getaways, hotel stays, or extended trips shouldn't mean giving up on pleasure. Our "Best for Travel" picks are specifically selected for their portability, discreet packaging, and reliability away from home. These compact devices fit easily in your carry-on, charge quickly, and deliver the same quality you expect from larger devices.

Whether you're staying at a boutique hotel, booking an Airbnb, or traveling on business, these products mean you can enjoy intimate moments without worrying about size or noise. Their small form factors eliminate bulk, while their rechargeable batteries mean you don't need to pack AA batteries (or remember to charge a device the night before).

What Makes These Great Travel Picks

  • Quick Charge — Power up in 30 minutes for immediate use

Travel Tips for Pleasure Products

Use a Travel Pouch

All three devices come with travel cases. The Lelo Gigi 2's leather pouch feels like premium luggage, while the Dame Eva II's fabric pouch fits in any bag. These cases protect the device and prevent it from rolling around in luggage.

Quick Charge Before Flying

Charge these devices before your flight. The Dame Eva II charges fully in 90 minutes, while the Satisfyer Pro 2 takes 2 hours. The Lelo Gigi 2 charges fastest at 1.5 hours. Bring your phone charger to power them from hotel outlets.

Keep in Carry-On

Never pack these in checked luggage. Temperature extremes damage batteries and materials. Keep them in your carry-on with easy access. The Satisfyer Pro 2's waterproof build means you can rinse it in hotel sinks.

Discreet Lube

Pack small bottles of water-based lube in your toiletry bag. The Dame Eva II's compact size means it fits in your handbag, while the Satisfyer Pro 2's travel pouch keeps everything organized in one bag.
