Our Affiliate Relationship

Last Updated: July 3, 2026

Venus Reviews participates in the Lovehoney Affiliate Program and other affiliate programs. This means that when you click on links to our partner retailers (including but not limited to Lovehoney and other premium pleasure product retailers) and make a purchase, we may earn a commission at no additional cost to you.

What This Means For You

For Our Readers

  • No Extra Cost: Our affiliate commissions come at no additional cost to you. The products cost the same whether you click our affiliate link or search for them directly.
  • Same Products: The products you receive are identical whether you purchase through our affiliate link or directly from the retailer.
  • Same Protections: You receive all the same benefits, warranties, and protections from the retailer regardless of how you purchase.

Why We Use Affiliate Links

  • Supporting Independent Review: Affiliate commissions help us maintain editorial independence from advertisers. Our reviews are not influenced by marketing budgets.
  • Free Content: Our affiliate program helps us provide high-quality, free content including product reviews, buying guides, and comparisons.
  • Transparency: FTC regulations require us to disclose affiliate relationships. We want you to know about them, not to hide them.

How Commissions Work

Commission Structure

Our commission rates typically range from 1% to 8% of the purchase price, depending on the retailer and product category. Some of our partners also offer higher commission rates for repeat sales or promotional periods.

Cookie Duration

Our affiliate cookies generally last between 30-90 days. This means if you click our affiliate link and make a purchase within that timeframe, we'll earn a commission even if you don't purchase immediately.

Maximum Value Commissions

Some of our partners offer "maximum value" or "super affiliate" programs that pay higher commissions (up to 15% or more) for top-performing affiliates. These programs are available to select partners but not to us.

How We Maintain Integrity

Editorial Independence

We are absolutely not influenced by our affiliate relationships. Our reviews are based on:

  • Hands-on product testing and research
  • Real-world performance evaluation
  • Independent analysis of features and benefits
  • Honest assessment of pros and cons

We may decline affiliate opportunities if we don't feel a product deserves our coverage. Editorial decisions are ours alone.
