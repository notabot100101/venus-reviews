<h1>Secure Transactions</h1>

<p>Venus Reviews is an editorial review site. We help readers compare products, privacy considerations, shipping expectations, and retailer trust signals before they buy.</p>

<p>When a review links to a retailer, complete your purchase only on the retailer's secure checkout page. Look for HTTPS, clear shipping policies, discreet billing language, and a support route before placing an order.</p>

<p>We avoid collecting sensitive personal details on this site. For purchase support, shipping questions, or billing issues, contact the retailer directly through their official support channel.</p>
