How to Choose the Right Product #

Buying a pleasure product can feel overwhelming. With hundreds of options across dozens of categories, where do you start?

This guide breaks down the key factors to consider, organized by what actually matters for your decision.

Updated July 22, 2026 with refreshed product-fit, privacy, and support checkpoints.

First: Know Your Priorities #

Before browsing products, ask yourself:

What’s Your Experience Level? #

  • First-time buyer? Start with approachable, intuitive designs
  • Some experience? Consider what you liked/disliked about previous products
  • Experienced user? You likely know what features matter most to you

What’s Your Budget? #

Tier Price Range What to Expect
Entry $50-$80 Basic features, simpler materials
Mid-range $80-$150 Better motors, body-safe silicone
Premium $150-$250 Top-tier materials, advanced features
Luxury $250+ Refined engineering, brand prestige

What’s Your Living Situation? #

  • Need discretion? Prioritize quiet operation and compact storage
  • Shared walls? Noise level becomes important
  • Solo living? You have more flexibility on size and storage

Key Features Explained #

Noise Level #

Measured in decibels (dB), but we categorize simply:

  • Whisper-quiet (under 40 dB): Barely audible from a few feet away
  • Quiet (40-50 dB): Similar to a quiet conversation
  • Moderate (50-60 dB): Noticeable but not disruptive
  • Loud (60+ dB): Comparable to a vacuum cleaner

Materials Matter #

Best: Medical-grade silicone — non-porous, hypoallergenic, easy to clean

Good: ABS plastic — body-safe, firm sensation

Avoid: Porous materials (jelly, rubber) — can harbor bacteria

Power Source #

  • Rechargeable: Convenient, eco-friendly, often stronger motors
  • Battery: Travel-friendly, no charging downtime
  • Plug-in: Unlimited power, but limited mobility

Product Types Overview #

External Stimulators #

Focus stimulation on external areas. Great for:

  • Beginners (less intimidating)
  • Partner play (easy to incorporate)
  • Travel (usually compact)

Internal Devices #

Designed for internal stimulation. Consider:

  • Size and girth preferences
  • Firmness vs. flexibility
  • Shape and curve design

Dual-Action (Rabbit Style) #

Combine internal and external stimulation simultaneously. Best for those who want both sensations in one device.

Couples Toys #

Designed for use during partner play. Look for:

  • Secure fit during movement
  • Controls accessible to both partners
  • Positions compatibility

Reading Reviews Effectively #

When browsing Venus Reviews, focus on:

  1. The “Who It’s For” section — Does it match your situation?
  2. The “Caveats” section — Are the downsides dealbreakers for you?
  3. Comparisons — How does it stack up to similar products?

Red Flags to Avoid #

  • No material listed — Reputable products always disclose materials
  • “Miracle claims” — Nothing works for everyone
  • Extreme pricing — Suspiciously cheap often means counterfeit
  • No reviews anywhere — Established brands have user feedback
